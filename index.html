What is U-Ferment?

U-Ferment (also called U-Vin or U-Brew) lets you make wine on our premises using professional equipment and our expertise. It's legal, it's affordable, and the results rival what you'd find on any store shelf. You pick a kit, start fermentation with us, and return in a few weeks to bottle your finished wine—approximately 30 bottles per batch, no experience needed.

How It Works

It's simple. Browse our selection and reserve a kit online or in store. Book a time that works for you, then come in to start your batch—we'll walk you through the process in about 20 minutes. Your wine ferments and clears in our facility over 4–8 weeks. When it's ready, you come back to bottle, cork, and label your creation—about 45 minutes. That's it—great wine, made by you.
